Third Coast Music

Third Coast Music is a company that I started working with on a freelance basis in 2016. First, I started by helping them with their logo after a name change. I took the logotype that TCM had and updated it using Adobe Illustrator and delivered multiple file formats that could be used for anything they may need in the future.

Then I helped the ladies of Third Coast Music build their website using SquareSpace. I helped them choose a design that was best suited for their content and product. I guided them as they curated their content and photos, and then taught them how to upload everything to SquareSpace.

After the launch of their website, Third Coast Music has needed various printed materials for self-promotion. I've designed and built these using Adobe InDesign or sometimes Photoshop.
