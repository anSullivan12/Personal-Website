Coffey Communications

Coffey Communications works with health care providers and health plan providers to communicate with their communities through printed pieces and websites. After I graduated from Bowling Green State University in 2011, I started working at Coffey Communications as a Web Designer. My responsibilities included designing and building websites for clients, designing new releases for Coffey's netReturns app, designing e-newsletters, designing support materials (printed and online) for the netReturns modules, and making updates to client websites based on client requests.

Coffey does not allow for any of their designers to post or share the work that they during their employment there. So sadly, I cannot share the four redesigns that I did for Coffey clients.
